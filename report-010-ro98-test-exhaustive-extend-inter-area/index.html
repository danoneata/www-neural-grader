Grading the 1998 Red Oak dataset

The 1998 data bank for kiln-dried red oak lumber consists of 3,487 boards which were graded automatically and then whose grades were refined manually. On this web-page we are working on a subset of boards (the 'test' split), and we are showing those boards that our algorithm graded differently than the groundtruth.
